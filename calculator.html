Time Conversion Calculator

Welcome to the Time Conversion Calculator! This calculator will convert radians to standard timekeeping units or system (hours, minutes, and seconds; or in 24 hour or 12 hour time) and vice versa. This calculator, however, does not convert between units in the standard timekeeping units or system (no hours to minutes or hours to 12 hour time, for example) or between 24 hour and 12 hour radians or vice versa. However, an easy way to convert 12 hour radian time to 24 hour radian time is to divide the time by 2, and add π if PM. Finally, remove the AM or PM. An easy way to do this is to turn π into a fraction with the same denominator as the new fraction; example: π/2 radians PM = (π/2)/2 + π = π/4 + 4π/4 = 5π/4 radians. To convert from 24 hour radian time to 12 hour radian time, one can substract pi if the original time is after π radians, and then multiply the time by 2. If the original time is on or after π radians, add PM, if original time is before π radians, then add AM. This can be done by making π the same denominator as the original fraction; example: 7π/4 radians = (7π/4 - π)*2 = (7π/4 - 4π/4)*2 = (3π/4)*2 = 3π/2 radians PM.
